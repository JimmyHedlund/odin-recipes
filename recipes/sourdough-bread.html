Sourdough bread - "country style"




Recipe

  • 80g mature starter
  • 300g water
  • 320g Manitoba cream
  • 80g whole grain spelt flour
  • 10g salt

Steps

  1. Combine water and flour and let it rest 30 min for the autolyze
  2. Add the starter to the dough and let it sit for another 30 min
  3. Add the salt to the dough along with a tbsp or two of water
  4. Fold the dough every 30 min for three folds anbd then let it rest for 2 hours
  5. Make ther final shape of the dough and put it in a basket and let it ferment i the fridge for atleast 6 hours
  6. Bake the bread at 250C for 20 min and then lower the oven to 220 for additional 20 min
